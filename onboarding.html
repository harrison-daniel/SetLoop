SetLoop

Control any video with your voice. Loop sections, change speed, bookmark moments — all without touching your keyboard.

1
Open any video
Navigate to YouTube, Vimeo, Coursera — any page with a video player.
2
Press Alt + V
Or click the SetLoop icon in your toolbar. This activates your microphone — a green dot confirms you're live.
3
Say a command
Try "loop last 30 at 50 percent" to loop the last 30 seconds at half speed. Say "stop" when done.
Voice Commands
"loop last 30 at 50%" Loop + slow down
"loop 1:30 to 2:00" Loop a range
"stop" End loop, restore speed
"speed 75" Set playback speed
"slower" / "faster" Adjust by 25%
"back 10" Rewind 10 seconds
"bookmark" Save this moment
"copy link" Copy timestamped URL

Privacy-first. Your mic is only active when you turn it on. SetLoop never records, stores, or sends any audio or data. Everything stays on your device.
